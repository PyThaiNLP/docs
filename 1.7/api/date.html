pythainlp.date¶

The pythainlp.date helps output dates, as spelled out in Thai.

pythainlp.date.now()[source]¶
Returns

the current date with Thai month and Thai year. The month is spelled out in text, and the year is converted from AD to Thai years. (ie: 30 ตุลาคม 2560 20:45:30)
